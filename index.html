Place that you Must Visit

I had visited this place in December 2018

Quaint little restaurant tucked inside a by-lane of Juhu. If you blink, you might miss the enterance to the cafe, so be alert to spot the black sign that says "Prithivi Cafe" while you are on the main road. This restaurant has a good menu for good prices, the food is delicious, the servings are pretty big, and some dishes can be shared with multiple people. The restaurant attracts a crowd of celebrities on show days. If you want to see them, book in advance to avoid dissapointments. This restaurant also hosts the famous "Prithivi Theater Fest", which you must attend if possible.

Avengers Endgame
Excellent film in the MCU Universe
